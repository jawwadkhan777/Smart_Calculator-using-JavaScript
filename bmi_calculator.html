BMI Calculator

Welcome to your personal BMI Calculator, your go-to tool for assessing your body mass index (BMI) and understanding your health status at a glance.
How to Use:
Input your age, height (in centimeters), and weight (in kilograms) into the designated fields.
Simply click the button "Calculate BMI" to generate your BMI result instantly.
What Results to Expect:
BMI Value: Your BMI will be displayed accurately with two decimal places, providing a precise measurement of your body mass index.
Interpretation: Alongside your BMI value, you'll receive a clear and concise message indicating whether you are underweight, healthy, overweight, or obese based on your calculated BMI.
Empower yourself with knowledge about your body composition and take proactive steps towards a healthier lifestyle with our BMI Calculator.
